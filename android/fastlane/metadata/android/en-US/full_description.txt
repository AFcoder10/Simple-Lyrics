Simple Lyrics is a minimalist and high-performance lyrics viewer that integrates seamlessly with your Android media playback.

Features:
- Real-time synced lyrics for your currently playing music.
- Support for multiple sources and syllable-level timing.
- Immersive, edge-to-edge UI with dynamic blur backgrounds.
- High-fidelity haptic feedback synchronized with the rhythm.
- 3D Parallax effects for a premium visual experience.
- Efficient caching for offline lyrics and artwork.
- 100% Open Source and privacy-focused.
