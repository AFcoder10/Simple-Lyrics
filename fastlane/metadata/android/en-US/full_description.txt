Simple Lyrics is a minimalist and high-performance lyrics viewer for Android that integrates seamlessly with your media playback to show real-time synced lyrics.

Features:
- Synced Lyrics: Real-time display for currently playing tracks.
- Premium UI: Fluid blur backgrounds and 3D parallax effects for a modern look.
- Haptic Feedback: Feel the rhythm with synchronized vibrations that move with the music.
- Offline First: Automatic caching for lyrics and artwork ensures you always have your content.
- Privacy Focused: 100% open source with no trackers, advertisements, or proprietary blobs.

Enjoy a clean, focused listening experience with Simple Lyrics.
